Intern Program

What We're Looking For

  • Individuals who are detail oriented, team oriented, and understanding of the startup ecosystem
  • Previous experience working with startups and motivation to learn more
  • Willing to think outside of the box in order to provide new ideas and directions in which The Connector could explore

Our internship program will provide an extensive education of the beginning stages startup company’s progress through and in most cases will see such idea’s go to market. They will also be able to form their own individual marketing schemes, investor preparation, and the process involved in the evolution of our client’s ideas. We hope to build a foundation of excellent background knowable of the startup ecosystem and our intern’s will be able to build upon their own individual visions in order to become leaders in the entrepreneurial world themselves.
