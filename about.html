How We Started

  • The
    Beginning

    June 2016

    Our team at The Connector began with founders Brendan Smith and Charles Morris working at Elysium Industries on the capital raising team. Through their work with Elysium they began to learn the ins and outs of the startup world from both the client side as well as the investor side. They realized that they had a knack of building trust with relationships especially with investors and that skill would lead them to the start of an idea.

  • A Company
    is Born

    December 2016

    This led to them being brought onto multiple other ventures in recent years and then the idea of The Connector came to fruition when they brought on fellow co-founders Andrew Myers and Joseph Menendez. They decided to put their networks, marketing schemes, and fundraising success into a vision.

  • Transition
    to Full
    Service

    February 2017

    Through countless discussions with Andreas Widmer and Bob Allard they agreed that our vision had the potential to completely change the landscape of the startup ecosystem. Our team would provide the resources entrepreneurs of all degrees the tools they would need to succeed. In February 2017 our team officially launched The Connector.

  • Phase Two
    Expansion

    Present

    We are currently in four different cities with plans to continue to build and develop our network on a global scale. We invite entrepreneurs in all industries, any age, and all you need is an idea. Our team will turn that idea into the reality you have in mind and can be as hands on or off as one desires. If you believe in your vision just like we did in ours then join us and create your story.
